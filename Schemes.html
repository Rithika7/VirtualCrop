Government Schemes

MIDH Telangana

The State Horticulture Mission (Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture) was launched on 3rd November, 2005, under National Horticulture Mission.

Micro Irrigation Project

Effective utilization of every drop of water through micro irrigation is imperative for improving crop productivity, production and to achieve sustainable improvement in living standards of small and marginal farmers of State by improving the water use efficiency through micro irrigation and farmers can get assured additional income.

Rastriya Krishi Vikasa Yojana

Rastriya Krishi Vikasa Yojana (RKVY) (Central Sector Scheme) has been launched 2007-08 in Erstwhile Andhra Pradesh State and implementation in Horticulture sector was started from 2008-09. Up to 2014-15 it was 100% grant from GOI, from 2015-16 onwards the funding pattern changed to 60:40 ratio of GOI and State Govt. respectively.

National Mission on Oil Seeds & Oil Palm

Government of India has launched National Mission on Oil Seeds & Oil Palm (NMOOP) during 2014-15, duly discontinuing RKVY (OP) and an Integrated Scheme of Oil seed, Pulses, Oil Palm and Maize (ISOPOM) was merged with NMOOP & now being implemented under National Food Security Mission - Oil Palm (NFSM-OP) from 2019-20.
